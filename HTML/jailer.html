JAILER

The movie "Jailer" is a 2023 Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Nelson Dilipkumar and produced by
Kalanithi Maran under the Sun Pictures banner. Here's a quick overview:

Key Details:

  • Cast:

    • Rajinikanth as Tiger Muthuvel Pandian (the titular "Jailer")
    • Vinayakan as the main antagonist
    • Ramya Krishnan, Vasanth Ravi, Yogi Babu, and others in supporting roles
    • Special appearances by Mohanlal, Shiva Rajkumar, and Jackie Shroff
  • Plot: The story revolves around Muthuvel Pandian, a retired jailer whose peaceful life is disrupted when he is drawn back into action to protect his family and deal with a dangerous gang.
  • Music: The soundtrack and score were composed by Anirudh Ravichander, whose music played a significant role in the film's appeal.
